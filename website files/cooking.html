Lasagna Rolls

Ingredients
Directions
Serves

Lasagna Noodles 1 Box

Spaghetti Sauce 24 oz

Your favorite brand or homemade

Ground Beef 1 lb

Sausage 1/2 lb

Pork or Turkey Sausage

Ricotta Cheese 24 oz

Shredded Italian Cheeses4 cups

Eggs 2

Garlic2 Cloves

Italian Seasoning 1 Tbsp

Garlic SaltCouple Shakes

PepperCouple Shakes

Cook Lasagna Noodles

Al dente - About 10 minutes - Drain and rinse, then place in cool water

Cook Beef and Sausage

Throw the beef and sausage into a skillet with 1 chopped up garlic clove, 1tsp italian seasoning, garlic salt, and pepper - Drain oil and set aside

Pre-heat Oven to 350 degrees

Mix Ricotta, egg, meat mixture, and 2 cups shredded cheese

Mix well and add 1 chopped up garlic clove, 1tsp italian seasoning, garlic salt, and pepper

Time to Roll

Line the bottom of a 13X9 baking pan with Spaghetti Sauce. Place a noodle flat in the sauce and spread with Ricotta filling (about 1/4 inch thick), top with sauce and roll - Repeat placing rolls 3 across and 4 to 5 down until you are out of noodles or room in the pan.

Top with sauce and cheese and throw in the oven

Pour the rest of the sauce on top and then top with the remaining shredded cheese. Put in the oven and cook for 30 minutes.

Serves 6 to 8

Place 1 or 2 rolls per plate

Serve with garlic cheese bread and some salad

ENJOY!
